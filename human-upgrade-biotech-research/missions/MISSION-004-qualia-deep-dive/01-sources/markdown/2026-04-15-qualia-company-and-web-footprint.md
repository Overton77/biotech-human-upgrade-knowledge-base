# Qualia company and web footprint

## Summary

Current official pages support `Qualia Life Sciences` as the active public-facing company and commerce identity, with the site footer and multiple live product pages using `Qualia Life Sciences, LLC`. A May 2024 official site article says `Neurohacker Collective` was becoming `Qualia Life Sciences` and would start operating under that DBA on `qualialife.com`, while later PR coverage surfaced in Tavily search results says the company completed a broader rename to `Qualia Life Sciences LLC` in September 2024. In this pass, the public website provides strong current-state brand and operations signals, but the exact registry-grade legal transition is still not independently verified.

## Strong Current Signals

- Current official domain: `https://www.qualialife.com/`
- Current public address: `2011 Palomar Airport Road, Suite 101 - PMB 160, Carlsbad, CA 92011`
- Current support contact: `support@qualialife.com`
- Current support phone: `(855) 281-2328`
- Current footer entity string: `Qualia Life Sciences, LLC`
- Founder / CEO signal from official FAQ: `James Schmachtenberger`

## Rebrand And Entity Structure

- Official site article dated `2024-05-21` says `Neurohacker Collective is becoming Qualia Life Sciences`.
- That article says the company would start operating under the `DBA Qualia Life Sciences` and that the ecommerce store would be hosted on `qualialife.com`.
- Current official pages and footer no longer present `Neurohacker Collective` as the dominant public brand.
- Current footer, people pages, and product pages use `Qualia Life Sciences, LLC`, which is a stronger current-state legal-style signal than the older DBA language.
- A clean independent registry or filing confirmation of the precise legal rename path was not completed in this slice.
