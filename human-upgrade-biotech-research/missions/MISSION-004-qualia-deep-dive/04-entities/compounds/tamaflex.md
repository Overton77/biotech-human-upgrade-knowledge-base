# TamaFlex

## Summary

`TamaFlex` is a branded blend of tamarind seed extract and turmeric rhizome extract used in [[qualia-joint-health]]. Official Qualia copy presents it as a patented blend used for mobility, flexibility, and joint-support outcomes.

## Current Mission-Relevant Facts

- Current Qualia Joint Health dose: `250 mg`
- Official composition signal: `Tamarind (Tamarindus indica) seed extract` + `Turmeric (Curcuma longa) rhizome extract`
- Official rationale: Qualia says it selected the ingredient as part of a clinically studied joint-health stack and ties dosage logic to published human research

## Evidence Read

- This is one of the clearest branded-ingredient evidence targets for the later PubMed verification stage.
- Current mission slice confirms active current use and dosage in `Qualia Joint Health`, but not yet the full sponsor / replication map.
