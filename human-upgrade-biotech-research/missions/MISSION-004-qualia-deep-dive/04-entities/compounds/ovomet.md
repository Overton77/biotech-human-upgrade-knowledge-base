# Ovomet

## Summary

`Ovomet` is a branded eggshell membrane ingredient used in [[qualia-joint-health]]. Official Qualia ingredient pages describe it as a Spanish-made eggshell-membrane ingredient containing undenatured collagen plus a broader biomolecule matrix including hyaluronic acid.

## Current Mission-Relevant Facts

- Current Qualia Joint Health dose: `300 mg`
- Official rationale: Qualia says it used the same amount as human studies referenced on the ingredient page
- Supplier / origin signal: made in Spain
- Composition signal on official page: `22%` undenatured collagen and about `9 mg` hyaluronic acid per serving

## Evidence Read

- Company presentation suggests this is one of the stronger current evidence-backed ingredients in `Qualia Joint Health`.
- The exact human evidence and independence of that evidence still need the dedicated PubMed pass.
