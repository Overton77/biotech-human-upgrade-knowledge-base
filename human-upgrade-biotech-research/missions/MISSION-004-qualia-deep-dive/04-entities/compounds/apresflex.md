# ApresFlex

## Summary

`ApresFlex` is a branded `Boswellia serrata` gum-resin extract used in [[qualia-joint-health]]. Official Qualia ingredient copy frames it as a relatively well-studied mobility-support ingredient with enhanced bioavailability and rapid-onset benefit signals in human studies.

## Current Mission-Relevant Facts

- Current Qualia Joint Health dose: `100 mg`
- Official standardization signal: not less than `20% AKBA`
- Official rationale: Qualia says it matched the `100 mg` amount used in the human studies it highlights
- Official supplier attribution signal: `PLT Health Solutions / Laila Nutraceuticals` branding appears in the broader seed material and supporting public references

## Evidence Read

- Among the three core `Joint Health` branded ingredients, `ApresFlex` appears to have one of the clearest company-articulated dose-to-study links.
- Full evidence-strength and sponsorship mapping still needs the later literature pass.
