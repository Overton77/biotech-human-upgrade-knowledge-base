# Timeline Web Footprint And Company Context

## Summary

- `timeline.com` is the active public-facing domain for both the nutrition and skincare businesses.
- Current official copy repeatedly frames Timeline as the consumer-facing brand and `Amazentis` as the parent company behind Mitopure.
- Timeline presents itself as a Swiss-born longevity brand rooted in EPFL-linked science, with `Mitopure` positioned as its proprietary Urolithin A technology.
- Official financing and launch materials state that `Nestle Health Science` has been an investor since 2019 and that `L'Oreal` joined via the January 2024 Series D round.

## Company Structure Signals

- Official Timeline copy says: `Timeline (parent company Amazentis)`.
- The about pages describe Timeline as a Swiss-born longevity brand founded by scientists, doctors, and innovators.
- Leadership pages identify a commercial operating layer branded as `Timeline Longevity` / `Timeline Longevity Inc.` while scientific-advisory copy continues to reference `Amazentis`.
- `Patrick Aebischer` is explicitly described as co-founder of Amazentis.
- `Anurag Singh` is described as Chief Medical Officer at Timeline with prior experience at Nestle / Nestle Health Science and at Amazentis/Timeline.

## Business Context

- The 2024 financing post states that Timeline raised `CHF 56M (USD 66M)` in an oversubscribed Series D round.
- The same post says the round included strategic investments from `L'Oreal` and `Nestle`, with Nestle already an investor since 2019.
- The 2026 Lancôme post says Timeline's Mitopure technology is being deployed in a Lancôme skincare line, extending Mitopure from supplements into partnered beauty commercialization.

## Notes On Evidence Quality

- Company-structure and investor claims above are primarily `Company-asserted`.
- The financing post is useful as a first-party business-context source but should not be treated as an independent filing.
- The Timeline / Amazentis relationship is well signaled by first-party language, but this pass did not establish a full legal-entity hierarchy from registry or filing records.
