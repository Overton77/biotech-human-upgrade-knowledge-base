# Timeline / Amazentis

## Overview

Timeline is the public-facing longevity brand commercializing `Mitopure`, while first-party materials repeatedly identify `Amazentis` as the parent company or originating scientific organization behind the platform.

## Current Identity Signals

- Swiss-born longevity brand
- built around proprietary Urolithin A ingredient `Mitopure`
- active in both nutrition and skincare
- investor / partner context includes `Nestle Health Science` and `L'Oreal`

## Leadership Signals

- Brian Ray, CEO
- Chris Rinsch, Co-founder & President
- Anurag Singh, Chief Medical Officer
- Federico Luna, Chief Marketing Officer

## Business Context

- January 2024 first-party post says Timeline raised `CHF 56M` in Series D financing.
- Nestle is described as an investor since 2019.
- L'Oreal joined the 2024 financing round and later partnered on skincare commercialization.

## Evidence Read

- Company science is stronger than average for a supplement brand.
