# Anurag Singh

## Overview

Anurag Singh is the current Chief Medical Officer at Timeline and one of the most important bridge figures between the company's scientific claims, clinical-trial program, and commercial product positioning.

## Role In This Mission

- executive and scientific spokesperson
- recurrent author on key Mitopure / Urolithin A papers
- central to immune-aging and muscle-health evidence interpretation

## Background Signals

- MD in internal medicine
- PhD in immunology
- prior work at Nestle / Nestle Health Science
- experience at Amazentis / Timeline
- described by Timeline as author on 40+ papers, 15+ patents, and 50+ randomized clinical trials

## Importance

Singh's repeated presence across product pages, studies, and company profiles strengthens the coherence of the Timeline evidence stack, but also highlights the commercial linkage of the evidence base.
