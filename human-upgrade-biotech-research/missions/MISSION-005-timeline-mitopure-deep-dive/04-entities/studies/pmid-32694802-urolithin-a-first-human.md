# PMID 32694802

## Summary

First-in-human trial showing that oral Urolithin A was safe, bioavailable, and associated with biomarker signatures consistent with improved mitochondrial and cellular health in sedentary older adults.

## Key Details

- Population: healthy sedentary older adults
- Dose: single-dose and multiple-dose arms, including `500 mg` and `1000 mg`
- Duration: 4-week multiple-dose phase
- Main readout type: safety, pharmacokinetics, and biomarker / gene-expression outcomes

## Interpretation

- Strong support for safety and bioavailability
- Moderate support for biologic activity
- Weak support for broad functional consumer claims on its own
