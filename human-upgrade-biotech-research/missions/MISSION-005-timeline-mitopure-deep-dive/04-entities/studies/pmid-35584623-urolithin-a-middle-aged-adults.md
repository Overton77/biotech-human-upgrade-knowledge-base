# PMID 35584623

## Summary

Randomized trial in overweight, low-endurance middle-aged adults reporting muscle-strength improvements and biomarker changes after 4 months of Urolithin A supplementation, with more mixed results on some primary performance endpoints.

## Key Details

- Population: middle-aged adults, roughly `40 to 65`, overweight and low-endurance
- Dose arms: `500 mg`, `1000 mg`, and placebo
- Duration: `4 months`
- Registry: `NCT03464500`

## Outcome Read

- Positive:
  - approximately `10% to 12%` muscle-strength improvements
  - improved biomarker patterns linked to mitochondrial health
  - some gains in VO2 and 6-minute walk measures
- Important nuance:
  - primary peak-power endpoint was not significant
  - stronger support for muscle / biomarker claims than for broad performance-superiority framing

## Interpretation

This is one of the best current public supports for Timeline's muscle-strength and exercise-performance language, but it remains a company-linked trial and should not be read as independent replication.
