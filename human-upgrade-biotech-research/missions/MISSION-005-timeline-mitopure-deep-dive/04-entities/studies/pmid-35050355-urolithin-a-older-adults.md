# PMID 35050355

## Summary

Randomized clinical trial in older adults showing that 1000 mg/day Urolithin A improved selected muscle-endurance measures and some plasma biomarkers over 4 months, while some broader performance outcomes were less definitive.

## Key Details

- Population: adults aged `65 to 90`
- Sample size: `66` randomized participants
- Dose: `1000 mg/day`
- Duration: `4 months`
- Journal: `JAMA Network Open`

## Outcome Read

- Positive:
  - improved endurance in repeated contraction tests
  - favorable changes in acylcarnitines, ceramides, and CRP-related biomarker signals
- More limited:
  - not all whole-body functional endpoints moved strongly
  - marketing shorthand can over-compress the nuance

## Interpretation

This is a meaningful human efficacy study and one of the strongest public anchors for Timeline's oral-muscle claims, but it does not justify sweeping anti-aging claims by itself.
