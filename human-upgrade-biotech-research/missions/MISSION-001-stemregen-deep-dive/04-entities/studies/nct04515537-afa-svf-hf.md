# NCT04515537 — AFA + SVF in Heart Failure

**"Cyanophyta Aphanizomenon flos-aquae, and Adipose Stroma Vascular Fraction, in Heart Failure Patient"**

## Critical Assessment

### Design Flaws

1. **Misclassified** — listed as "observational" on ClinicalTrials.gov but described as "randomized controlled"
2. **Grossly underpowered** — ~5 patients per arm
3. **No true control group** — all arms receive active intervention
4. **Missing protocol details** — SVF delivery route not specified
5. **Outcome measure confusion** — uses SAQ-7 (angina questionnaire) to assess NYHA class (heart failure)

### Sponsor Independence — Not Demonstrated

- Sponsor ("Sociedad Española de Medicina Regenerativa y Terapia Celular") has **zero publications** and only 2 registered trials
- Investigators use personal Gmail addresses
- Clinic is a private practice
- **Sister trial NCT05699694** (Parkinson's) by same investigators **explicitly names "StemRegen product"** as intervention

### STEMREGEN/Kalyagen Connection

The AFA dosing (6 capsules/day) matches STEMREGEN product dosing. The sister trial (NCT05699694) directly names the STEMREGEN product. The AFA product in NCT04515537 is **almost certainly a Kalyagen product** with the brand name undisclosed in the heart failure trial registration.

## The 2025 CHF Publication

A publication titled "The Effect of Endogenous Stem Cell Mobilization on Congestive Heart Failure" in *Regenerative Medicine* was mentioned in company materials. **Not found on PubMed** as of April 2026. May be in a non-indexed journal, still in press, or may not exist as described.
