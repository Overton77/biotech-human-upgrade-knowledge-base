# Viome Company Foundations

## Bottom Line

Viome appears to be a private, venture-backed precision-health company with a dual public footprint: a consumer-facing `viome.com` commerce brand and a research / diagnostics-facing `viomelifesciences.com` layer. Its current public positioning blends longevity, microbiome health, RNA-based systems biology, early diagnostics, and personalized nutrition.

## Organization Picture

- Consumer / brand layer: `viome.com`
- Research and grants layer: `viomelifesciences.com`
- Diagnostic subdomain / offer: `cancerdetect.viome.com`
- Public leadership remains centered on [[naveen-jain|Naveen Jain]] with long-running scientific leadership from Guruduth Banavar and Momo Vuyisich.
- Public pages emphasize multidisciplinary work across microbiology, clinical practice, bioinformatics, AI, epigenetics, and systems biology.

## Leadership Snapshot

### Current named executives

- [[naveen-jain|Naveen Jain]]: Founder and CEO
- Guruduth Banavar, PhD: Founding CTO and Head of Discovery AI
- Momo Vuyisich, PhD: Founding CSO and Head of Clinical Research
- Shannon Pappas: President, Consumer Business
- Helene Vollbracht: Chief Product Strategy Officer, Corporate Brand and Communications
- Michael Bass, MD: Chief Medical Officer
- Craig Pazarena: VP of Finance

### Advisory / board signal

Company pages currently surface prominent academic / medical names including Alessio Fasano, George Church, Andrew McCallum, Salomon Amar, Umadevi Naidoo, Ed Zuckerberg, and Sanjiv Chopra. This improves the credibility of the network around Viome, but it should still be treated as `Company-asserted` until independently confirmed role-by-role.

## Business Context

- `Company-asserted`: Viome frames itself as a prevention, diagnostics, and precision-nutrition platform.
- `Press / private-market`: Tavily search results surfaced 2022-2024 funding coverage describing a `$67M` Series C extension, later `~$86.5M` total Series C reporting, and a `2024` `$25M` internal Series D tied to a diagnostics push and Kal Raman joining as COO.
- `Financial filing`: Edgar search did not surface a meaningful SEC company match for Viome, which supports treating Viome as private for this mission.

## Operational / Regulatory Signal

- Product pages and diagnostic pages repeatedly reference a `CLIA-certified` lab.
- CancerDetect is explicitly described as `not FDA-approved or cleared` on the current public pages.
- Public blog and diagnostic pages also state the oral / throat cancer tool received FDA `Breakthrough Device` designation for accelerated review, which should be treated as a distinct claim from clearance or approval.

## Current Strategic Lanes

1. Consumer testing and recommendation engine
2. Personalized supplements and oral / gut biotic products
3. Research-services / grant platform for external collaborators
4. Early diagnostics, especially oral / throat cancer

## Assessment

### Stronger conclusions

- Viome is currently active across both consumer and research-facing domains.
- Its public product strategy still centers on at-home testing plus downstream personalized interventions.
- Diagnostics, especially oral / throat cancer detection, remain a major strategic story and not just a legacy press-release artifact.

### Caveats

- Revenue, headcount, and detailed legal-entity structure were not independently verified in this mission.
- Funding figures are press-reported rather than SEC-filed.
- Company pages mix consumer-wellness language with stronger medical / disease language; those categories should remain analytically separate.
