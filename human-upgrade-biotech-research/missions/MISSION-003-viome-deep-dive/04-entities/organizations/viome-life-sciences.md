# Viome Life Sciences

Viome Life Sciences is a private precision-health company that combines at-home RNA-based microbiome and human gene-expression testing with personalized recommendations, supplements, oral-care products, and an oral / throat cancer diagnostic-aid program.

## Summary

- Public domains in this mission: `viome.com`, `viomelifesciences.com`, `cancerdetect.viome.com`
- Core public pillars: consumer testing, personalized products, grants / research services, and early diagnostics
- Founder and CEO: [[naveen-jain]]
- Strongest current program differentiation: RNA / metatranscriptomic platform plus oral-health and cancer-detection positioning

## Business / Regulatory Context

- Viome appears private in this mission; Edgar did not surface a clear SEC company footprint.
- Press coverage points to multiple venture rounds and a 2024 diagnostics push.
- CancerDetect is marketed with FDA Breakthrough Device language but also explicitly states it is not FDA-approved or cleared.
- Public pages repeatedly state sample processing occurs in a `CLIA-certified` lab.

## Assessment

Viome is not just a gut-test brand. The current ecosystem spans microbiome scoring, human-gene-expression analytics, oral-health profiling, personalized interventions, and an active oral-cancer detection program. The platform and diagnostics story are stronger than the downstream wellness claims.
