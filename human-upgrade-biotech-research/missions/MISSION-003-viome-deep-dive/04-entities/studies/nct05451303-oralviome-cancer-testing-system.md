# Detection of Oral and Throat Cancers Using OralViome Cancer Testing System

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|

| Status | `RECRUITING` |

## Why it matters

- This is one of the key registry records showing that Viome's oral-cancer program is an active sponsored study rather than just a marketing claim.
- It directly evaluates the `OralViome Cancer Testing System` using saliva metatranscriptomics.
- It links the published `CDOT` evidence lane to an ongoing clinical-validation program.

## Trial Read

- The study evaluates design, safety, and efficacy of the OralViome Cancer Testing System for early detection of oral and throat cancers.
- The intervention includes the saliva-collection kit, Viome lab metatranscriptomic processing, and Viome analytical software.
- Cohorts include `OSCC`, `OPC`, oral potentially malignant disease, and cancer-free controls.

## Viome linkage

- Sponsor is `Viome`.
- Contacts include `Momchilo Vuyisich, PhD` and `Mory Mehrtash`.
- The record is one of the clearest sponsored-study signals in the Viome mission.

## Caveats

- Registry presence does not equal posted results or successful clinical adoption.
- This is an ongoing sponsored observational study and should be paired with the published paper and later results tracking.
