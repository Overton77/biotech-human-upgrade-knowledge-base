# Validity of Viome's Oral/Throat Cancer Test

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| NCT ID | `NCT06174428` |
| Status | `RECRUITING` |
| Design | observational |
| Enrollment | `1000` |
| Sponsor | `Viome` |

## Why it matters

- This is the larger current validation record for Viome's oral / throat cancer test.
- It shows the cancer-detection program is still being actively refined and evaluated on clinically suspicious cases.
- It is one of the best registry anchors for the `CancerDetect` evidence lane.

## Trial Read

- Participants are adults with clinical suspicion of `OSCC` or `OPSCC`.
- The device is intended as a diagnostic aid for qualitative detection of molecular features in saliva samples.
- Primary outcomes include sensitivity, specificity, positive post-test probability, and negative post-test probability.

## Viome linkage

- Sponsor is `Viome`.
- Contacts again include `Momchilo Vuyisich` and `Mory Mehrtash`.
- This is a stronger late-stage validation signal than a generic discovery registry.

## Caveats

- The study is still recruiting and outcome data are not yet posted in the registry.
- It supports active validation status, not a final settled clinical-efficacy conclusion.
