# Efficacy of Viome's Precision Nutrition Program Towards Reducing HbA1c

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| NCT ID | `NCT05465616` |
| Status | `RECRUITING` |
| Design | randomized placebo-controlled interventional study |
| Enrollment | `150` |
| Sponsor | `Viome` |

## Why it matters

- This is one of the strongest registry objects for Viome's precision-nutrition clinical-utility story.
- It goes beyond platform methods and evaluates whether a Viome-guided program can affect glycemic outcomes.
- It is important for separating ongoing outcome research from already-proven efficacy.

## Trial Read

- Adults with `HbA1c` between `5.7%` and `8.9%` are randomized into placebo or Viome Precision Nutrition Program arms.
- The intervention can include nutrition, diet, app-based guidance, and precision supplements based on microbiome results.
- Primary outcomes include `HbA1c` and insulin resistance over `4 months`.

## Viome linkage

- Sponsor is `Viome`.
- Contacts include `Momchilo Vuyisich` and `Mory Mehrtash`.
- This is directly tied to Viome's precision-nutrition program rather than only to the platform infrastructure.

## Caveats

- The study is still recruiting, so it supports active research status rather than completed efficacy.
- Because the intervention is multi-component, future interpretation will need care around attribution.
