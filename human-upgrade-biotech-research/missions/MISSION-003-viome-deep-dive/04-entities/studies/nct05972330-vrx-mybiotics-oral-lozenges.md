# Efficacy of VRx MyBiotics Oral Lozenges

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| NCT ID | `NCT05972330` |
| Status | `COMPLETED` |
| Design | interventional |
| Enrollment | `34` |
| Sponsor | `Viome` |

## Why it matters

- This is the clearest sponsored registry object tied directly to one of Viome's named personalized oral products.
- It shows Viome did move beyond oral-health theory into a product-specific interventional study.
- It is especially useful for grounding the `VRx MyBiotics Oral Lozenges` claim set.

## Trial Read

- The study tested personalized oral-biotic lozenges in adults with oral-microbiome-health outcomes in view.
- Participants received a `30-day` supply of personalized oral-biotics lozenges.
- The primary outcome was `Viome Oral Health Score` over roughly `4 months`.

## Viome linkage

- Sponsor is `Viome`.
- The intervention is Viome's own `VRx MyBiotics Oral Lozenges` product.
- The study site is `Viome Life Sciences` in Bothell, Washington.

## Caveats

- Small enrollment (`n=34`) limits confidence.
- The registry record is useful but remains a company-sponsored product study without posted high-level results in this mission pass.
