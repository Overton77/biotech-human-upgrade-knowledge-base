# Viome Official Company Pages

## Summary

- `viome.com/company` and `viomelifesciences.com/about-us` present Viome as a private precision-health and longevity company centered on RNA-based microbiome and human gene-expression analysis.
- The current public leadership layer includes [[naveen-jain|Naveen Jain]], Guruduth Banavar, Momo Vuyisich, Stephen Barrie, Helene Vollbracht, Michael Bass, Shannon Pappas, and Craig Pazarena.
- The advisory and board pages emphasize academic and investor credibility, but these are still company-controlled representations and should not be treated as independent validation.
- `viomelifesciences.com/grants` positions the research arm as a systems-biology and diagnostics platform offering in-kind grants, sample-kit logistics, lab/bioinformatics analysis, machine learning, and IRB support.

## Company-Asserted Details

### Organization framing

- Viome describes its method as finding "a signal in the noise" across health data.
- The public mission framing centers on chronic disease, cancer, and aging prevention.
- The company says recommendations are built from `600,000+` samples on the company page and `1 million+ tests analyzed` on current product pages, so the exact current benchmark should be treated carefully as a moving company claim.

### Leadership and scientific structure

- Founder and CEO: [[naveen-jain|Naveen Jain]]
- Founding CTO and Head of Discovery AI: Guruduth Banavar, PhD
- Founding CSO and Head of Clinical Research: Momo Vuyisich, PhD
- VP and Head of Clinical Nutrition: Stephen Barrie, ND, PhD
- Chief Product Strategy Officer, Corporate Brand and Communications: Helene Vollbracht
- Chief Medical Officer: Michael Bass, MD
- President, Consumer Business: Shannon Pappas
- VP of Finance: Craig Pazarena

### Grants and research-services layer

The grants page says Viome can provide:

- sample kits for blood, stool, saliva, urine, plasma, CSF, throat swabs, vaginal swabs, and surfaces
- laboratory and bioinformatics analyses including strain-level taxonomy and quantitative gene expression
- machine-learning support
- digital recruitment and consent infrastructure
- federally accredited IRB support in the U.S.

## Provenance Notes

- These pages are strong for current self-description, leadership rosters, and current operational framing.
- They are not independent evidence for efficacy, analytical validity, or business performance.
- The grants page is especially useful for understanding what sample types and service capabilities Viome publicly claims to support.
