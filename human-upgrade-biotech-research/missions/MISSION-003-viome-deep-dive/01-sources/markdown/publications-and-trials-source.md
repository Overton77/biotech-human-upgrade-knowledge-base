# Viome Publications And Trial Registry Source Pack

## Summary

- Viome's own publications page currently lists a meaningful body of metatranscriptomic and precision-nutrition papers from `2019` through `2026`.
- PubMed confirms multiple company-linked methods and validation papers tied to saliva metatranscriptomics, blood transcriptomics, oral-health assessments, and control-material development.
- ClinicalTrials.gov shows that Viome is not just citing research rhetorically; it is also listed as sponsor on multiple observational and interventional studies.

## Key PubMed-Verified Papers

| PMID | Year | Topic | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| `32772558` | 2020 | capillary blood transcriptome test | finger-prick blood, ambient-temp transport up to 4 weeks |
| `36622006` | 2023 | saliva metatranscriptomic test | `<100 ul` saliva, ambient-temp storage up to 28 days |
| `37454545` | 2023 | oral / throat cancer detection | validation cohort for CDOT / CancerDetect oral-throat paper |
| `38328005` | 2024 | saliva-based health assessments | oral pathway scores tied to periodontitis, acid reflux, nicotine addiction |
| `38319053` | 2024 | subgingival sampling proxy method | supports oral metatranscriptomic sampling workflow |
| `40128554` | 2025 | synthetic positive controls | CLIA-lab control-material support for MT diagnostics |

## Key ClinicalTrials.gov Records

| NCT ID | Title | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| `NCT05451303` | Detection of Oral and Throat Cancers Using OralViome Cancer Testing System | Recruiting | observational saliva-metatranscriptomic cancer-screening study |
| `NCT06174428` | Validity of Viome's Oral/throat Cancer Test | Recruiting | larger current oral/throat validation study targeting sensitivity/specificity |
