# Viome Current Product Catalog And Test Pages

## Summary

- The current catalog is centered on three consumer test products, one at-home cancer-screening-oriented diagnostic aid, and several personalized product lines that depend on prior testing.
- Current list pricing captured from the shop page is `$399` for [[full-body-intelligence]], `$279` for [[gut-intelligence]], `$259` for [[oral-health-intelligence]], and `$599` starting price for [[cancerdetect-oral-throat]].
- Personalized products are marketed as downstream formulas rather than standalone diagnostic products: Precision Supplements, VRx My·Biotics Gut Formula, VRx My·Biotics Oral Lozenges, and VRx My·Biotics Toothpaste & Gel.
- The exact ingredient composition of personalized formulas is not publicly fixed because Viome describes them as individualized; only ingredient classes and example strains/actives are publicly surfaced.

## Personalized Products

### Precision Supplements

- marketed as custom capsules with `50+` possible personalized ingredients blended into `8 capsules daily`
- categories publicly named: vitamins, minerals, herbs, enzymes, amino acids, and botanicals
- Viome says ingredient selection uses health scores, clinical-trial dosages, and life stage
- exact formula composition is individualized and not public in a fixed SKU format

### VRx My·Biotics Gut Formula

- positioned as personalized prebiotic and probiotic support for digestive health
- Viome says strain choice depends on Gut Health scores and microbial activity
- public examples include `Bacillus clausii CSI08`, `Bifidobacterium breve Bb-03`, and `Partially Hydrolyzed Guar Gum`
- shelf-stability and no-refrigeration claims are public, but full SKU-level composition is personalized

### VRx My·Biotics Oral Lozenges

- described as personalized oral prebiotic, probiotic, and postbiotic lozenges
- public product page says formulas include a minimum of `30 billion` total beneficial oral biotics
- public example strains / ingredients include `Streptococcus salivarius`, `Streptococcus salivarius M18`, `Lactobacillus acidophilus`, `Ligilactobacillus salivarius`, and `KT-11 (Lactobacillus crispatus postbiotic)`

### VRx My·Biotics Toothpaste & Gel

- public actives and positioning emphasize oral-biotic personalization plus evidence-backed actives
- explicitly named actives include `Methylsulfonylmethane (MSM)` and `Sodium Tripolyphosphate (STPP)` for the gel
- toothpaste copy says formulas can include personalized postbiotics, prebiotic fibers, and polyphenols based on Oral Health scores

## Important Framing Notes

- Company pages mix consumer wellness language with stronger disease-prevention language. Those should not be flattened into one evidence tier.
- The personalized products are not meaningfully comparable to ordinary fixed-formula supplements because the company says formulas adapt after testing and re-testing.
- Oral and cancer products are the strongest examples of Viome exposing named sample types and performance-oriented language publicly.
