## Current Findings

- Viome currently presents as a private precision-health platform spanning consumer testing, personalized interventions, research services, and oral / throat cancer diagnostics.
- The live catalog centers on `Full Body Intelligence`, `Gut Intelligence`, `Oral Health Intelligence`, and `CancerDetect`, with personalized supplements and oral / gut biotics downstream of testing.
- The strongest evidence supports platform logistics / methods and the CancerDetect program; broader wellness and precision-nutrition claims remain more mixed and often company-linked.
- ClinicalTrials.gov confirms active Viome-sponsored studies across oral-cancer detection, oral lozenges, and precision-nutrition outcome programs.
