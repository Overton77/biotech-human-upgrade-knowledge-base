# OMNi-BiOTiC Mission Synthesis

## Bottom Line

OMNi-BiOTiC is a private microbiome brand ecosystem centered on `Institut AllergoSan` in Graz, Austria, with a family-led `AllergoSan USA` / `OMNi-BiOTiC USA` commercial layer launched in 2019. After upgrading this mission, the evidence hierarchy is much clearer: `Stress Release` is the strongest U.S. flagship product by publication depth, `Hetox`, `Panda`, `Power`, `Caricol`, and `Caricol Gastro` also have meaningful PubMed-backed anchors, while `AB 10` remains commercially important but still relies more heavily on company-curated evidence summaries than the strongest formulations.

## Company Picture

- Institut AllergoSan was founded in 1991 and presents itself as a microbiome research and natural-medicine R&D company.
- The company emphasizes a family leadership model and long-term scientific collaboration.
- Official pages point to around 40-country reach and a market-leader position in Austria/Germany.
- The U.S. operation is explicitly framed as a family-led New York team run by Klaus, Hannah, and Lena Kleinfeld.
- Revenue is not publicly disclosed on the official sites, so directory estimates should be labeled as such and not blended across the Austrian parent and U.S. layer.

## Product Architecture

The live U.S. catalog clusters into four practical buckets:

1. Multi-strain probiotics
2. Prebiotics
3. Digestive aids
4. Bundles / programs that sequence existing formulas

The catalog is not just "a probiotic company"; it is a portfolio of:

- stress / gut-brain formulas
- antibiotic restoration formulas
- immune / allergy-related formulas
- gut-liver and cardiometabolic formulas
- maternal/neonatal formulas
- athletic-performance formulas
- prebiotic fiber support
- papaya-based digestive aids

## Evidence Quality Assessment

### Stronger Evidence Areas

- `Stress Release` has the best PubMed-verified evidence stack in the mission: [[bagga-2018-emotional-brain-signatures]], [[bagga-2019-resting-state-connectivity]], [[moser-2019-ibsd-synbiotic]], and [[leblhuber-2018-alzheimers-dementia]], plus the dementia registry `NCT03847714`.
- `Hetox` is backed by PubMed-verified metabolic literature including [[horvath-2020-diabesity-synbiotic]] and [[sabico-2017-t2dm-endotoxin]], though exact formulation matching should still be handled carefully.
- `Panda` has a clear flagship randomized trial in [[niers-2009-panda-eczema]].
- `Power` has a strong athlete-focused randomized trial in [[strasser-2016-power-athletes-urti]].
- `Caricol` and `Caricol Gastro` have direct digestive-support RCTs in [[muss-2013-caricol-digestive-disorders]] and [[weiser-2018-caricol-gastro-gastritis]].

### More Carefully Framed Areas

- `AB 10` remains commercially central and plausibly evidence-backed, but its best-known AAD story is still more company-summary dependent than the strongest flagship lines above.
- `Balance` looks more like a legacy immune / GALT formulation with older evidence and rationale.
- Bundles should be treated as curated combinations rather than new formulas.
- Company citations frequently mix study results, product marketing, and educational claims in the same page.

### Caveats

- The research pages are company-controlled.
- Some study summaries are company descriptions of published work rather than the publication text itself.
- Some formulas are described as identical to European originals; where direct formula matching was not fully confirmed in this mission, that should remain explicit.
- Third-party operational metrics are directory estimates, not audited figures.

## Trial / Registry Signal

Search results surfaced multiple relevant registrations, suggesting the broader brand ecosystem extends beyond the current U.S. retail lineup:

- vaginal microbiome and bacterial vaginosis studies
- endometriosis / fertility studies
- kidney transplantation
- colorectal cancer adjunctive probiotics
- dementia
- stroke and post-COVID / upper respiratory adjacencies outside the core current U.S. catalog

That means the research program is broader than the retail catalog and should be tracked separately when appropriate.
