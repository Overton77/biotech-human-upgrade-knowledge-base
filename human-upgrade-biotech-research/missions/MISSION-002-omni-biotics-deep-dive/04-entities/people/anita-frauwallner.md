# Anita Frauwallner

Anita Frauwallner is the founder and long-time leader of Institut AllergoSan, the Austrian company behind OMNi-BiOTiC.

## Summary

- Took over management of Institut AllergoSan in 1992
- Helped steer the company toward probiotic and microbiome-centered products
- Central figure in the company’s family-led leadership model
- Frequently cited by the company as its research and strategic leader
