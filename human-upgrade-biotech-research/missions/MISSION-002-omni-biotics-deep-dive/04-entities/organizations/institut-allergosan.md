# Institut AllergoSan

Institut AllergoSan is the Austrian parent organization behind the OMNi-BiOTiC ecosystem. It is based in Graz, Austria and presents itself as a microbiome research and natural-medicine company.

## Summary

- Founded in January 1991
- Family-led leadership model
- Focused on probiotics, prebiotics, plant extracts, and minerals
- Operates as a research and development center with international collaborations
- Active in approximately 40 countries

## Leadership

- Anita Frauwallner
- Bernd Assinger

## Research Positioning

- Official pages emphasize collaboration with clinics, universities, and research centers
- The company frames its research as microbiome-centered and prevention-focused
- The official research hub groups studies by indication rather than by academic discipline
