# OMNi-BiOTiC / AllergoSan USA

OMNi-BiOTiC / AllergoSan USA is the U.S. retail and brand-facing layer of the broader Institut AllergoSan ecosystem. It sells probiotics, prebiotics, digestive aids, bundles, and related accessories through `omnibioticlife.com`.

## Summary

- U.S. business launched in `2019`
- U.S. leadership includes Klaus Kleinfeld, Hannah Kleinfeld, and Lena Kleinfeld
- The U.S. site includes a named scientific advisory board / expert network
- The brand emphasizes gut-brain, gut-liver, immune, maternal/neonatal, athletic-performance, and restoration use cases
- The current product catalog is organized around targeted formulas and bundles rather than a generic probiotic SKU set

## Team / Story Context

- Klaus Kleinfeld is presented as the CEO and co-founder of the U.S. business with his daughters.
- Hannah Kleinfeld is presented as COO with healthcare strategy and operations experience.
- Lena Kleinfeld is presented as CMO and communications lead.
- The official site explicitly frames the U.S. operation as a family-led New York team.
