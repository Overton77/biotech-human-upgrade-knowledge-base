# Caricol-Gastro in Chronic Gastritis

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| PMID | `29604620` |
| Design | Randomized double-blind placebo-controlled trial |
| Population | `60` patients with mild chronic gastritis |
| Duration | `30 days` |
| Main endpoint | Disease-associated pain and symptom burden |

## Main findings

- Both groups improved, suggesting a meaningful placebo effect.
- Pain reduction was significantly greater in the Caricol-Gastro group (`p = 0.048`).

## Why it matters

- Strongest direct clinical evidence note for [[caricol-gastro]].
- Gives the mission a non-probiotic product with a clearly PubMed-indexed RCT.

## Caveats

- Add-on support trial rather than stand-alone disease treatment.
- Benefit was present but modest.
