# Papaya Preparation (Caricol) in Digestive Disorders

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| PMID | `23524622` |
| Design | Double-blind placebo-controlled study |
| Intervention | `20 ml` daily for `40 days` |
| Population | Volunteers with chronic digestive dysfunction symptoms |
| Main endpoints | Constipation, bloating, heartburn, bowel-movement pain |

## Main findings

- Significant improvements in constipation and bloating in the verum group.
- Heartburn trended positively but did not clearly reach significance in the smaller subgroup with that symptom.

## Why it matters

- Strongest direct human evidence note for [[caricol]].

## Caveats

- Symptom-focused digestive-support trial rather than a disease-treatment study.
