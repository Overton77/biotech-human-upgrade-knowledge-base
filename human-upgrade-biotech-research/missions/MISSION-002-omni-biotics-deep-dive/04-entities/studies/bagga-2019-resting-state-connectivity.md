# Influence of 4-week Multi-Strain Probiotic Administration on Resting-State Functional Connectivity

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| PMID | `29850990` |
| Design | Double-blind randomized pre/post intervention study |
| Population | `45` healthy volunteers |
| Duration | `4 weeks` |
| Main method | Resting-state and diffusion MRI |

## Why it matters

- One of the clearest follow-on studies to the 2018 Bagga gut-brain paper.
- Strengthens the Stress Release evidence stack with neuroimaging rather than only behavioral endpoints.

## Main findings

- Significant probiotic-associated changes were reported in the default mode, salience, and frontal networks.
- No significant structural-diffusion differences were found.

## Caveats

- Healthy-volunteer mechanistic / physiology trial, not a patient-outcome trial.
- Supports brain-gut plausibility more than direct therapeutic claims.
