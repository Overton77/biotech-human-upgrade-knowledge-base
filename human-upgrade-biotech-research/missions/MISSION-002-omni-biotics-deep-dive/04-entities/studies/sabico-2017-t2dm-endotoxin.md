# Multi-Strain Probiotic in Medication-Naive T2DM

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| PMID | `29228964` |
| NCT | `NCT01765517` |
| Design | Randomized clinical trial |
| Population | `78` medication-naive Saudi T2DM patients in published analysis |
| Duration | `12/13 weeks` published analysis |
| Main endpoint | Circulating endotoxin and cardiometabolic markers |

## Main findings

- No significant between-group difference in endotoxin levels.
- Significant improvement in HOMA-IR and modest improvement in abdominal adiposity markers.
- Useful as a metabolic-support signal, but not a clean "endotoxin solved" story.

## Why it matters

- Adds a registry-backed PubMed-verified evidence line to the Hetox / metabolic cluster.

## Caveats

- Exact product equivalence to current U.S. Hetox branding should be stated cautiously.
- Outcomes were mixed, not uniformly positive.
