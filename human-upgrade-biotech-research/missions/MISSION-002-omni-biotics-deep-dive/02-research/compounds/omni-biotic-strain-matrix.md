# OMNi-BiOTiC Strain and Compound Matrix

## Scope

This note treats recurring probiotic strains and reusable non-strain support ingredients as the mission’s practical `compound` layer.

## Most Reused Strains Across the U.S. Catalog

## Product Families by Strain Architecture

### Gut-brain family

- `Stress Release`
- `Stress Release Kids`
- Characteristic pattern: `W56`, `W19`, `W22`, `W52`, `W20`, `W62`, `W51`, `W23`, `W24`

### Antibiotic-restoration family

- `AB 10`
- `AB 10 Kids`
- Characteristic pattern: `W55`, `W20`, `W37`, `W71`, `W1`, `W24`, `W51`, `W62`, `W54`, `W23`

### Gut-liver / metabolic family

- `Hetox`
- Characteristic pattern centers on `W37`, `W23`, `W63`, `W52`, `W56`, `W19`, `W24`, `W58`, and in some product documentation `W51`

### Infant / allergy family

- `Panda`
- Characteristic pattern: `W58`, `W52`, `W51`, `W23`

### Athletic-performance family

- `Power`
- Characteristic pattern: `W54`, `W22`, `W63`, `W58`, `W23`, `W51`

## Interpretation

- OMNi-BiOTiC does not sell isolated-strain products in the U.S. catalog; it sells repeated multi-strain architectures tuned to different use cases.
- The strongest product evidence often supports a `strain system` or named European formulation rather than a single bacterial strain.
- For this mission, strain reuse is important because it links products across categories and helps explain why some evidence can plausibly transfer between adjacent formulas while still requiring careful formulation-matching language.
