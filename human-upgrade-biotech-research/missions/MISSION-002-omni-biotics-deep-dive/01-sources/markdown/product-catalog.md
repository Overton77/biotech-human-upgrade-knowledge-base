## Product-Level Research Hooks

- Stress Release and the Dynamic Duo connect most directly to gut-brain axis research.
- AB 10 and the Gut Health Reset Program connect most directly to antibiotic-associated diarrhea and microbiome restoration research.
- Balance connects to immune/GALT and allergy-related literature.
- Hetox connects to gut-liver axis, endotoxemia, and cardiometabolic literature.
- Panda connects to maternal/neonatal and allergy-prevention literature.
- Power connects to athletic performance and oxidative stress literature.
- Caricol and Caricol Gastro connect to papaya digestive-enzyme and gastritis / reflux literature.
- Omni-Logic products are the main prebiotic support products and should be treated as nutrient/fiber formulas rather than probiotics.

## Notes

- The current live catalog is narrower than some broader brand pages and should be treated as the present U.S. retail set.
- Bundle pages should be used as evidence of sequencing or pairing, not as novel ingredient formulas.
