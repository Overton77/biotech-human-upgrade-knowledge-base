# TruHealth

## Summary

`TruHealth` is TruDiagnostic's systems-health and nutritional biomarker product. Public pages frame it as an at-home finger-prick test that reports broad biomarker families related to nutrition, metabolism, immune function, inflammation, neurocognition, toxins, oxidative stress, and related health domains.

## Publicly Exposed Details

- Intended user: consumer and provider-ordered patient testing
- Specimen: finger-prick blood for consumer kits; provider page also permits lavender-top EDTA blood
- Public price: `$499` one-time on shop pages
- Turnaround: `3-4 weeks` after lab receipt
- Public biomarker-count language:
  - `105+` biomarkers
  - `110+` biomarkers
  - `+75` antioxidants, vitamins, or fatty acids in some packaging language
- Public report families:
  - nutrient and vitamin levels
  - antioxidant levels
  - amino acid levels
  - metabolic health
  - energy levels
  - inflammation levels
  - stress markers
  - neurocognitive markers
  - immune function health
  - cardiovascular health
  - toxin exposure
  - heavy metal exposure

## Example Named Outputs
