# TruAge + TruHealth

## Summary

`TruAge + TruHealth` is TruDiagnostic's combined consumer package that bundles the aging-focused TruAge layer with the systems-health TruHealth layer.

## Publicly Exposed Details

- Intended user: consumer
- Specimen: finger-prick blood
- Public price: `$849` one-time, `$2,649` annual 4-kit subscription
- Turnaround: `3-4 weeks` after lab receipt
- Public package framing:
  - `180+` blood biomarkers analyzed
  - biological age, pace of aging, organ-age, nutrient, metabolic, inflammation, toxin, neurocognitive, and cardiovascular coverage

## Cautions

- The combined package inherits the same public count inconsistencies as its component products.
- Public biomarker totals are best treated as company-asserted packaging claims until reconciled at the per-analyte level.
