# TruAge

## Summary

`TruAge` is TruDiagnostic's core consumer-facing epigenetic aging test. Public pages position it as a finger-prick blood test built on methylation analysis across `1 million+` CpG sites and packaged around biological-age, pace-of-aging, organ-age, and related risk-style outputs.

## Publicly Exposed Details

- Intended user: consumer and provider-ordered patient testing
- Specimen: finger-prick blood for consumer kits; provider page also permits lavender-top EDTA blood
- Public price: `$499` one-time on shop pages
- Turnaround: `2-3 weeks`, `3-4 weeks`, and older FAQ-style `4-6 weeks` all appear publicly
- Core outputs:
  - OMICmAge
  - DunedinPACE
  - SYMPHONYAge
  - telomere length
  - 12 Cell Immunity
  - inflammation score
  - smoking impact
  - alcohol impact
  - type 2 diabetes risk
  - cancer risk
  - mortality risk
  - grip strength and gait speed / fitness-style outputs

## Cautions

- `[UNVERIFIED]` The current commercial distinction between `TruAge` and `TruAge COMPLETE` is not fully clean across public pages.
- Public turnaround and output language are not fully consistent across marketing, FAQ, and provider surfaces.
