# DunedinPACE reporting

## Summary

`DunedinPACE` is the pace-of-aging report layer TruDiagnostic bundles into TruAge-related reporting and publicly frames as the current speed at which the body is aging.

## Publicly Exposed Details

- Public role: pace-of-aging report within TruAge and provider-facing reporting
- Public interpretation example:
  - a score of `0.8` means the body is aging `0.8` years for each calendar year
- Public framing:
  - licensed DunedinPACE algorithm
  - based on the Dunedin longitudinal cohort
  - described as a third-generation biological clock in marketing copy

## Cautions

- The commercial report packaging should be separated from the underlying published DunedinPACE literature.
- Marketing statements such as `only third-generation biological clock` should remain company-asserted until validated against the broader clock landscape.
