# TruDiagnostic research and turnkey services

## Summary

TruDiagnostic publicly presents a research-service and turnkey-study line for academic, clinical, and industry collaborators. This layer includes methylation arrays, sequencing services, bioinformatics, multi-omic integration, and study-support language.

## Publicly Exposed Details

- Intended users:
  - academic researchers
  - healthcare providers
  - clinical institutions
  - industry leaders
- Accepted sample types:
  - blood
  - saliva
  - solid tissue
- Public platform language:
  - Infinium Methylation Screening Array
  - Infinium MethylationEPIC v2.0
  - custom arrays
  - whole-genome bisulfite sequencing
  - Illumina GSA v4 genotyping
- Public service language:
  - array sequencing
  - WGBS
  - bioinformatics
  - multi-omic integration
  - manuscript support
  - intervention-efficacy framing
  - methylation risk scores and deconvolution
- Public commercial signal:
  - relative claim of up to `$50` cheaper per sample than university labs

## Cautions

- Public pages do not provide a clean current pricing schedule or exact data-delivery terms.
- The research-service line overlaps with the company's grant, interventional-trials, and publications narrative and should be kept separate from consumer-test marketing.
