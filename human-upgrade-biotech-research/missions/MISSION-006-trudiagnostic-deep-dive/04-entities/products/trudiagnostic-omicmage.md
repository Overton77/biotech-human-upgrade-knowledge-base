# OMICmAge

## Summary

`OMICmAge` is TruDiagnostic's flagship biological-age output and algorithm family, positioned as a more predictive multi-omic aging score than chronological age alone.

## Publicly Exposed Details

- Public role: biological age score used within TruAge-related reporting
- Public pricing signal: `$15` digital upgrade for eligible `TruAge COMPLETE` customers on the OMICmAge page
- Measurement framing:
  - biological age score
  - multi-omic integration language
  - methylation risk scores for proteins, metabolites, and clinical biomarkers
- Public claims:
  - nearly `90%` predictive accuracy language
  - example comparison of `88.9%` versus `75.6%` chronological-age baseline
  - research-linked positioning through the company research page

## Cautions

- `OMICmAge` is also rendered as `OMICAge` on some public pages.
- Public claims around predictive accuracy should remain company-asserted until tied to specific published evidence and exact study context.
