# OMICmAge 2026

## Summary

This peer-reviewed `Nature Aging` paper is the strongest current evidence object for the `OMICmAge` family. It models EMR-linked biological aging with DNA methylation and multi-omic signals and includes a TruDiagnostic validation cohort.

## Key Details

- PMID: `41741793`
- DOI: `10.1038/s43587-026-01073-7`
- Population:
  - discovery cohort from Mass General Brigham Aging Biobank
  - validation cohorts include TruDiagnostic and Generation Scotland
- Core metric:
  - OMICmAge
  - DNAmEMRAge
- Endpoints:
  - incident and prevalent chronic diseases
  - mortality association

## TruDiagnostic Tie

- TruDiagnostic appears in author affiliations.
- A TruDiagnostic cohort is used as a validation cohort.

## Evidence Interpretation

- Supports predictive and association claims for the OMICmAge framework.
- Does not automatically validate every commercial interpretation layer used in TruDiagnostic marketing.
