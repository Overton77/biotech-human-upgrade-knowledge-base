# NCT05294835

## Summary

This registry record is one of the clearest examples of TruDiagnostic directly sponsoring an intervention study that uses epigenetic age as a primary outcome.

## Key Details

- NCT ID: `NCT05294835`
- Sponsor: `TruDiagnostic`
- Status: `UNKNOWN`
- Study design:
  - prospective clinical pilot study
  - phase 2
  - n = 20
- Population:
  - adults with MDD or PTSD
- Intervention:
  - ketamine infusion
- Primary endpoint:
  - epigenetic age change from baseline to study completion

## TruDiagnostic Tie

- TruDiagnostic is the listed sponsor.
- The summary explicitly references the `TruAge` epigenetic age laboratory test.

## Evidence Interpretation

- Strong registry evidence of study intent.
- No publication linkage was confirmed in this pass.
