# Senolytic Non-Reversal 2026

## Summary

This `Aging Cell` paper is a high-value limitation signal for the mission because it argues that methylation signatures of cellular senescence do not reverse after senolytic treatment, challenging simplistic intervention-tracking narratives.

## Key Details

- PMID: `41746138`
- DOI: `10.1111/acel.70430`
- Focus:
  - overlap between senescence-linked CpGs, age, and mortality risk
  - whether senolytic treatment reduces these methylation signatures
- Main finding:
  - the predictors stayed the same or even accelerated after senolytic treatment
