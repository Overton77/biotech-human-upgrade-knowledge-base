# DunedinPACE 2022

## Summary

This `eLife` paper is the strongest independent anchor for TruDiagnostic's `DunedinPACE` reporting. It introduces a next-generation DNA-methylation biomarker of pace of aging with high test-retest reliability and morbidity, disability, and mortality associations.

## Key Details

- PMID: `35029144`
- DOI: `10.7554/eLife.73420`
- Study design:
  - biomarker development from the Dunedin longitudinal cohort
  - additional validation in five datasets
- Core endpoint:
  - pace of aging distilled into a blood-based methylation biomarker

## TruDiagnostic Tie

- No TruDiagnostic authorship is required for the core biomarker paper.
- TruDiagnostic uses this algorithm commercially in TruAge reporting.

## Evidence Interpretation

- Strong support for the underlying algorithm family.
- Marketing claims about exclusivity or commercial superiority should still be evaluated separately.
