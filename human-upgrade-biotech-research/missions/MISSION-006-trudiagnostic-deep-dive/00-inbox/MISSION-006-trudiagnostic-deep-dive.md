## Current Findings

- TruDiagnostic currently presents as a Lexington, Kentucky-based epigenetic diagnostics company with consumer, provider, research, and commercial-partnership lines.
- Official pages support a CLIA-lab and research-platform positioning, not just a simple DTC testing brand.
- The mission now has a durable current-state product and service inventory covering `TruAge`, `TruHealth`, `TruAge + TruHealth`, `OMICmAge`, `SYMPHONYAge`, `DunedinPACE`, and research-service lines.
- The biomarker and report inventory is substantially improved by public support-center content and a downloadable sample TruAge PDF, including named analytes, organ-system lists, and retest guidance.
- The official `rules` page provides an on-site legal-name signal for `TruDiagnostic, Inc.`, while a Kentucky court document uses the variant `Tru Diagnostics, Inc.`
- PubMed verification supports OMICmAge, DunedinPACE, Systems Age / SYMPHONYAge-adjacent methodology, a senolytic limitation paper, and EPIC v2 platform context.
- ClinicalTrials.gov shows a meaningful TruDiagnostic-sponsored study set for intervention tracking, including ketamine, fasting-mimicking, device, and sleep-supplement studies.
- Current public materials support NIH-backed R&D activity and partnership expansion; a confirmed SEC or Form D trail was not established in this pass.
