# TruDiagnostic product and service catalog

## Summary

Current public TruDiagnostic offerings span direct-to-consumer testing, provider-facing diagnostic ordering, pharmacogenetic and cancer-risk add-ons, and research-service or turnkey-study support. The clearest current consumer SKUs are the shop-hosted `TruAge + TruHealth`, `TruHealth`, and `TruAge` products.

## Product-Level Notes

### TruAge

- Primary consumer-aging product.
- Public pages tie it to OMICmAge, DunedinPACE, SYMPHONYAge, telomere length, immune-cell and inflammation outputs, plus risk-style reports.
- A help-center page says the `TruAge COMPLETE` kit adds immune-aging and specific-gene-methylation insights beyond baseline biological-age reporting.

### TruHealth

- Primary systems-health and nutritional biomarker product.
- Public language alternates between `105+` biomarkers, `110+` biomarkers, and category-style claims.
- Sample-report and product pages expose large biomarker families rather than a single flat official analyte list.

### TruAge + TruHealth

- Publicly presented as the broadest combined package.
- Shop pages and homepage copy say `180+` blood biomarkers are analyzed.

### Research / turnkey services

- Public routes suggest this is a meaningful line of business, not just a marketing afterthought.
- The most explicit service language is on `our-services`, `research`, `science`, `trudiscovery-grant`, and `interventional-trials`.

## Public Contradictions To Preserve

- TruHealth is described as `105+`, `110+`, and in some contexts `150+` biomarkers.
- TruAge turnaround is described as `2-3 weeks`, `3-4 weeks`, and on older FAQ-style language `4-6 weeks`.
- TruAge price appears both as a one-time `$499` shop listing and a marketing-page subscription-style lower number.
- Public reporting around `TruAge` versus `TruAge COMPLETE` remains partially conflated across pages.
