# TruDiagnostic official company pages: team, provider, research, and services

## Summary

Official TruDiagnostic pages present the company as a CLIA-certified epigenetic testing company with consumer, provider, research, and commercial-partner business lines. The strongest current pages for company fundamentals are `team`, `providers`, `research`, `our-services`, `commercial-partners`, `science`, and the shop-hosted 2021 CLIA announcement.

## High-Signal Official Claims

| Page | Evidence Type | Key points |
|------|---------------|------------|
| `team` | Company-asserted | Ryan Smith is described as founder. The team page also exposes current role labels including `Chief Executive Officer`, `Chief Experience Officer`, `Chief Technology Officer`, `Global Head of Research & Dev.`, `Director of Clinical Testing`, `Director of Bioinformatics and Principal Investigator`, `Clinical Genetics`, and `Chief of Staff`. |
| `team` | Company-asserted | TruDiagnostic describes itself as a CLIA-certified lab and health-data company focused on epigenetic methylation data. The page states the company started in 2020, had tested `15,000+` patients on the EPIC850k array, and had `30+` clinical trials. |
