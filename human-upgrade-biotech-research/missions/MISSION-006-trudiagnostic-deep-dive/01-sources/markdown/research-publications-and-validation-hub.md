# TruDiagnostic research, publications, and validation hub

## Summary

TruDiagnostic's public research layer includes peer-reviewed papers, preprints, grant announcements, and interventional-study marketing pages. Independent validation is strongest for `DunedinPACE`, while `OMICmAge` and `Systems Age` / `SYMPHONYAge` have both company-linked and broader academic research footprints.

## Evidence-quality interpretation

- Strongest independent evidence layer: `DunedinPACE`
- Strongest company-linked publication layer: `OMICmAge`
- Strongest organ-systems-age evidence layer: `Systems Age`, which likely underlies or informs `SYMPHONYAge` marketing
- Strongest analytical-validity / methods layer: `MethylationEPIC v2` evaluation paper
- Strongest cautionary intervention-interpretation layer: senolytic non-reversal paper

## Important gaps

- No clean PubMed hit was found for the exact company-marketing headline about `a vegan diet slows aging faster than an omnivore diet` in this pass.
- The exact publication status of several company-highlighted intervention stories remains unclear.
- Registry presence does not prove results reporting or publication.
