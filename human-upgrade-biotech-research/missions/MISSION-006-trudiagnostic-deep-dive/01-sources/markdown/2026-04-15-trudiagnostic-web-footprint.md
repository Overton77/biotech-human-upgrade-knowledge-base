# TruDiagnostic web footprint and mapped public routes

## Summary

Tavily mapping of `trudiagnostic.com` surfaced a meaningful current public site structure for company, provider, research, and product-adjacent pages. This gives a reliable first-pass inventory of public routes for Stage 1 and Stage 2 research.

## Mapped Routes

Primary pages discovered on `trudiagnostic.com`:

- `/`
- `/team`
- `/science`
- `/research`
- `/providers`
- `/our-services`
- `/commercial-partners`
- `/truage`
- `/omicmage`
- `/dunedin-pace`
- `/about-tests`
- `/interventional-trials`
- `/trudiscovery-grant`
- `/provider-application`
- `/commercial-partnership-interest`
- `/research-interest`
- `/careers`
- `/requisition-form`
- policy or support pages including `/privacy-policy`, `/terms-of-service`, `/returns-and-refunds`, and `/fcoi-policy`

## Observations

- The main marketing domain carries both consumer and B2B or research-facing content.
- Public routes suggest at least four major business lines:
  - consumer testing
  - provider workflows
  - research or academic services
  - commercial or distributor partnerships
- `shop.trudiagnostic.com` did not return mapped URLs in this Tavily pass, despite known live product and press-release pages. This likely means the shop requires deeper crawl or more interactive handling.
- Tavily map rejected `https://info.trudiagnostic.com` as an invalid start URL, so the active extent of the `info` subdomain remains unresolved in this pass.

## Research Value

- `team`, `research`, `providers`, `our-services`, and `commercial-partners` are the strongest Stage 1 company-fundamentals pages.
- `truage`, `omicmage`, `dunedin-pace`, and `about-tests` are high-priority Stage 2 product and biomarker pages.
- `interventional-trials` and `trudiscovery-grant` are high-priority Stage 3 evidence and business-context pages.
