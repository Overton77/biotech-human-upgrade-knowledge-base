# TruDiagnostic evidence map

## Framing

TruDiagnostic's public evidence surface combines:

- independent published clock literature used commercially
- company-linked papers with TruDiagnostic authors or cohorts
- registry-listed intervention studies sponsored by TruDiagnostic
- marketing headlines that may summarize papers or studies more aggressively than the underlying evidence supports

The mission should separate these layers rather than treat them as equivalent.

## Claim-cluster evidence map

| Claim cluster | Main evidence objects | Evidence category | Strength | Key caveat |
|------|------|------|------|------|
| DNA methylation clocks and biological-age estimation | [[pmid-41741793-omicmage]], [[pmid-35029144-dunedinpace]], [[pmid-40954326-systems-age]] | Peer-reviewed plus company-linked | supported | Commercial implementation is not identical to every underlying academic clock paper |
| Pace-of-aging metrics | [[pmid-35029144-dunedinpace]] | Independent peer-reviewed | supported | Strong for the algorithm family; weaker for every marketing claim around superiority or actionability |
| Organ-specific aging clocks | [[pmid-40954326-systems-age]], [[pmid-37503069-systems-age-preprint]] | Peer-reviewed / preprint | supported | `SYMPHONYAge` branding may be company packaging of adjacent or underlying methods rather than a separately validated public product paper |
| Multi-omic integration and OMICmAge | [[pmid-41741793-omicmage]], [[pmid-37904959-omicmage-preprint]] | Company-linked peer-reviewed / preprint | supported | Evidence is strong for association and predictive utility within the published cohorts, but still company-linked |
| Analytical platform and assay language | [[pmid-38455390-methylationepic-v2]] | Peer-reviewed methods paper | supported | Validates EPIC v2 platform characteristics, not the whole commercial TruDiagnostic report stack |

## Sponsorship and conflict signals

### Clear TruDiagnostic links

- OMICmAge papers include TruDiagnostic-affiliated authors and a TruDiagnostic validation cohort.
- Senolytic non-reversal paper includes TruDiagnostic affiliation.
- MethylationEPIC v2 platform paper includes TruDiagnostic affiliation.
- Several ClinicalTrials.gov records list `TruDiagnostic` directly as sponsor.

### Independent anchors

- DunedinPACE core paper does not require TruDiagnostic to establish the algorithm.
- Systems Age / organ-systems-age papers resolve independently in PubMed, even if TruDiagnostic may operationalize adjacent concepts commercially.

## Clinical utility vs analytical validity vs conceptual relevance

| Evidence object | Best fit |
|------|------|
| DunedinPACE 2022 | Analytical and prognostic validity for the pace-of-aging biomarker family |
| OMICmAge 2026 | Predictive biological-age framework with disease and mortality associations; not a blanket proof of every commercial use case |
| Systems Age 2025 | Organ-systems-age conceptual and predictive framework |
| MethylationEPIC v2 2023 | Analytical platform and assay-support context |
| Senolytic non-reversal 2026 | Limitation and interpretation evidence |
| TruDiagnostic-sponsored registry studies | Study-intent evidence only unless paired with results publications |

## Highest-value open questions

- Which published paper most directly validates the current branded `SYMPHONYAge` commercial implementation?
- Which TruDiagnostic-sponsored NCT studies have results or follow-on publications?
- Which TruHealth outputs are directly measured versus estimated from methylation-based proxies?
- How much of the current consumer product stack depends on company-only report logic rather than publicly described validated models?
