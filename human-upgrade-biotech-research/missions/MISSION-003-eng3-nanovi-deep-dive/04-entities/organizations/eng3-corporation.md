## Company Positioning

Eng3 presents itself as a medical-device company using biophysics and water-science concepts to support protein folding, oxidative-stress recovery, and repair-related cellular functions. The company markets the NanoVi platform as a device-based intervention rather than a pharmaceutical, supplement, or chemical therapy.

## Core Product Architecture

The current device family is centered on:

- [[nanovi-eco|NanoVi Eco]]
- [[nanovi-pro|NanoVi Pro]]
- [[nanovi-exo|NanoVi Exo]]

Each device is positioned as the same underlying technology delivered at different output levels and session durations.

## Business Model Signals

- Pricing is not publicly listed on the main site because of reseller agreements.
- The company uses direct inquiry, international resellers, and referral-program mechanics rather than a transparent e-commerce catalog.
- A 2024 referral letter discloses referral fees of `$300` for Eco, `$500` for Pro, and `$1,000` for Exo, suggesting a premium hardware sales model.
