## Positioning

Eng3 presents the Eco as the most economical home-entry model and the longest-session version of the NanoVi platform.

## Technical Notes

- Shared spectral-emission range: `1,100-1,300 nm`
- Excitation elements: `6`
- Water requirement: `250 ml` distilled water
- Minimum lifespan: `10,000 hours`

## Evidence Context

The Eco is not presented as mechanistically distinct from the other NanoVi models. The main difference is lower output and longer session time, so the evidence base is mostly platform-level rather than Eco-specific.
