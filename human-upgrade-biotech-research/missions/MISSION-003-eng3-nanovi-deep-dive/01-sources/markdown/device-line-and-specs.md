## Product Line Snapshot

Eng3 currently centers the NanoVi family on three device tiers:

- `NanoVi Eco`
- `NanoVi Pro`
- `NanoVi Exo`

The official overview frames them as the same core technology delivered at different output levels and session durations.

## Specs Table

| Device | Article number | Recommended session | Relative output | Smartcard system | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| NanoVi Eco | `4650-00` | `60 minutes or more` | `100%` baseline | No | `8.5 lb / 3.9 kg` | Entry/home model |
| NanoVi Pro | `4800-00` | `30 minutes or more` | `200%` of Eco | Yes | `9.0 lb / 4.1 kg` | Mid-tier, clinic/family sharing use |
| NanoVi Exo | `4900-00` | `15 minutes or more` | `400%` of Eco, `2x` Pro | Yes | `9.5 lb / 4.3 kg` | Highest-output model |

## Positioning by Model

- `Eco` is presented as the most economical home-entry device.
- `Pro` is described as the balance between price and performance, suitable for home or clinic settings.
- `Exo` is marketed as the most powerful option, especially for athletes, time-constrained private users, and professional centers.

## Pricing Posture

- Public pricing is not shown on the official site.
- The FAQ says prices are withheld due to agreements with international resellers.
- The `how-to-purchase` page routes users into direct inquiry rather than a public shopping-cart catalog.

## Commercial Signals from the Referral Letter

The 2024 referral-letter PDF does not disclose retail pricing, but it does disclose referral payouts:

| Device | Referral fee |
|---|---|
| NanoVi Eco | `$300` |
| NanoVi Pro | `$500` |
| NanoVi Exo | `$1,000` |

These are not retail prices, but they imply a materially tiered hardware sales model.

## Regulatory and Use Framing

- The FAQ states that NanoVi is a `Medical Device` registered with the US FDA and European regulatory counterpart for certain settings.
- The FAQ also distinguishes between medical-device power-cord requirements and a `NanoVi-Wellness` configuration for household use.
- Official pages pair the products with claims around oxidative stress, protein folding, recovery, wellness, and performance support rather than explicit disease-treatment claims.

## Notes

- The public marketing site is stronger on comparative positioning than on direct specs; the technical-spec PDF and manuals are the most useful spec-grade artifacts.
- For mission synthesis, treat `Eco`, `Pro`, and `Exo` as one platform differentiated by output, time efficiency, and commercial positioning rather than by radically different mechanisms.
