## Current Findings

- Eng3 is a private Seattle-based company founded in `2003` by `Hans Eng`, with `Rowena Gates` as a long-running public-facing executive and business-development lead.
- The official domain architecture is clear enough to route follow-up work: company/about pages, device overview, manuals, FAQ, studies hub, and professional-resource gateway.
- The NanoVi product family is best understood as a shared platform with three hardware tiers: `Eco`, `Pro`, and `Exo`.
- The strongest externally verified evidence in this mission is the 2022 `IJMS` mechanistic paper on coherent humidity and enzyme activity (`PMID 35054784`).
- The strongest human-study object is `NCT03630419`, a tiny MCI/AD registry-backed proof-of-concept combining a Mito-Food Plan with cellular repair therapy.
- Many additional athletic, HRV, oxidative-stress, and practitioner claims remain company-summary-driven rather than strongly publication-backed.
