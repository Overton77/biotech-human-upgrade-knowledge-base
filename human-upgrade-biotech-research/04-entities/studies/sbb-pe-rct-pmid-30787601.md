# SBB-PE Stem Cell Mobilization RCT

**"Rapid and selective mobilization of specific stem cell types after consumption of a polyphenol-rich extract from sea buckthorn berries (Hippophae) in healthy human subjects"**

Drapeau C, Benson KF, Jensen GS. *Clinical Interventions in Aging*, 2019;14:253–263.

## Study Overview

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Design | Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, **crossover** |
| Registration | NCT03388073 |
| Population | 12 healthy adults (8F, 4M), mean age 49.3 ± 21 years |
| Intervention | 500 mg SBB-PE (proanthocyanidin-rich water extract, sea buckthorn berries) |
| Placebo | Rice flour capsules |
| Washout | ≥1 week between crossover visits |
| Blood draws | Baseline, 1 hour, 2 hours |
| Primary outcome | Change in circulating stem cell subpopulations (flow cytometry) |
| Lab | NIS Labs, Klamath Falls, OR |
| Extract supplier | Puredia (Irvine, CA) |
| Funding | [[christian-drapeau|Drapeau]] affiliated with Biomx Stemceuticals |

## Key Results

| Stem Cell Population | Phenotype | Peak Change (SBB-PE) | P-value |
|---------------------|-----------|---------------------|---------|
| Progenitor cells | CD45^dim CD34+ CD309− | **+24.2% ± 5.3%** | P < 0.001 |
| Endothelial cells | CD45− CD31+ CD309+ | **+33.4% ± 10.2%** | P < 0.007 |
| MSC-like cells | CD45− CD90+ (n=8 only) | **+20.8% ± 5.7%** | P < 0.016 |
| Pluripotential cells | CD45^dim CD34+ CD309+ | **+19.5% ± 7.0%** | P < 0.016 |

"Peak change" = each individual's best response at either 1h or 2h (post-hoc analytical approach).

## Strengths

1. Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled crossover — gold standard for small pharmacodynamic studies
2. Prospectively registered (NCT03388073)
3. Controlled for circadian variation, stress, exercise confounders
4. Triplicate samples with high event counts (300K–600K events)
5. Multi-marker immunophenotyping (4 distinct populations)

## Limitations

1. **Very small sample (n=12; n=8 for CD90)** — pilot/proof-of-concept only
2. **"Peak response" analysis is post-hoc** — selecting each individual's best time point inflates effect sizes
3. **Single dose, 2-hour follow-up** — no sustained dosing, no tissue homing, no clinical outcomes
4. **Surrogate endpoint only** — circulating cell counts ≠ therapeutic benefit
5. **No absolute cell counts reported** — only percentage changes from baseline
6. **No correction for multiple comparisons** (4 populations × multiple timepoints)
7. **Statistical methods rudimentary** — paired t-tests in Excel; no mixed-effects models or period/sequence analysis
8. **No dose-response data** — single 500mg dose only
9. **No independent replication** — all human SBB-PE data from this single study

## Extract vs. STEMREGEN Release

The paper tests **500 mg SBB-PE as a standalone ingredient**. [[stemregen-release|STEMREGEN Release]] is a **7-ingredient blend** with undisclosed dose of sea buckthorn extract. The extract source (Puredia vs. current supplier) and extraction method may differ. **The paper does not validate the finished retail product.**

## Citation Impact

Only **9 citations** in 7 years (low impact). Most notable citing paper: PMID 39907413 (2025) — tests actual Stemregen product in rat periodontitis model.

## Evidence Quality Rating

**Level: Low (Pilot/Proof-of-Concept)**

Appropriate as hypothesis-generating; insufficient to support clinical claims about the retail product.
