## Controversies & Regulatory Issues

1. **MLM origins** — STEMTech International operated as multi-level marketing; primary distribution for early stem cell supplement work
2. **STEMTech v. Drapeau lawsuit (2016)** — Breach of contract, trade secret allegations; court denied injunction; STEMTech failed to establish claims
3. **Prop 65 settlement (2015)** — STEMTech products contained lead without warnings; $118K settlement
4. **Predatory journals** — Some publications in OMICS/Longdom journals (FTC fined OMICS $50.1M for deceptive practices)
5. **Structural conflict of interest** — Researches his own products, tested by collaborator's contract lab (NIS Labs)
