# Gitte S. Jensen, PhD

**Founder & Research Director**, NIS Labs (Natural Immune Systems Labs), Klamath Falls, Oregon.

## Role in STEMREGEN Research

Primary research collaborator of [[christian-drapeau|Christian Drapeau]]. Co-author on at least 5 PubMed-indexed papers including the foundational ESCM hypothesis (PMID 12208182) and the SBB-PE RCT (PMID 30787601). Her lab (NIS Labs) performs the flow cytometry assays used to measure stem cell mobilization for Drapeau's products.

## Conflict of Interest Note

NIS Labs is a **contract research organization** that tests nutraceuticals for industry clients. Jensen is both a scientific collaborator and a service provider — her lab is paid to test the products that Drapeau's companies sell. This is common in supplement research but represents a structural conflict of interest.
