## Evidence Assessment

Mobilize is the most pharmacologically conventional product in the line. Many ingredients (NAC, ginkgo, nattokinase, beetroot nitrate, L-citrulline) have substantial independent evidence for their individual claimed mechanisms (antioxidant, fibrinolytic, vasodilatory). The glycocalyx support concept (fucoidan + glucosamine) is more novel and less clinically validated. No published study tests the Mobilize formulation as a whole.
