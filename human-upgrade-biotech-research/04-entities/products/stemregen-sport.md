## Formula Difference from Release

Release SPORT replaces **fractionated colostrum** with **pterostilbene** (from *Pterocarpus marsupium* bark, 98%). This swap likely enables the NSF Certified for Sport designation, as colostrum contains growth factors that may be flagged under anti-doping protocols.
