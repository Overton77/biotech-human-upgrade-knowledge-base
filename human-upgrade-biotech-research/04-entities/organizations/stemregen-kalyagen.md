### Key Events

1. **2005:** Drapeau co-founds STEMTech with Ray Carter to sell StemEnhance (AFA extract) via MLM
2. **2015:** Drapeau departs STEMTech; founds Biomx Stemceuticals
3. **2016 (March):** Files Biomics LLC in Texas
4. **2016 (July):** STEMTech sues Drapeau — court denies injunction
5. **2017:** STEMTech files Chapter 11 bankruptcy
6. **2018:** STEMTech v. Drapeau case terminated
7. **2021:** Form D SEC filing — raises $564K
8. **2024:** Inc. 5000 recognition; Ryan Riley becomes CEO
9. **2025:** Inc. 5000 again; continued growth

## Evidence Posture

- Products marketed as **dietary supplements** — standard FDA disclaimers
- Research based on **small crossover trials** (n=12) of individual ingredients by [[christian-drapeau|Drapeau]] and [[gitte-jensen|Jensen]]
- **No published clinical outcome trials** of the finished multi-ingredient products
- One animal study (PMID 39907413) tests actual Stemregen product (rat model, 2025)
- Company-asserted trials in CHF and Parkinson's — results not yet in peer-reviewed literature
